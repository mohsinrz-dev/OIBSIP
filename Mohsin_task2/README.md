# Ratan Tata — A Legacy Beyond Measure
### A Premium Digital Tribute Website

A cinematic, luxury-styled tribute website dedicated to the life, achievements, and enduring legacy of Ratan Naval Tata (1937–2024). Designed to feel like a premium digital documentary — not a basic college project.

---

## What It Does

This is a fully self-contained, single-file tribute website that tells the story of Ratan Tata through rich storytelling, premium animations, and an editorial layout. It covers:

- **Hero Section** — Full-screen cinematic intro with his name, dates, tagline, and a signature quote
- **About Section** — Early life, education, and entry into the Tata Group
- **Career Timeline** — Six major milestones from 1961 to 2024, with alternating slide-in cards
- **Achievements Section** — Six landmark achievements including Tata Nano, Jaguar Land Rover, Tetley, TCS, Padma Vibhushan, and Air India
- **Live Counters** — Animated number counters for revenue, countries, employees, and companies
- **Philanthropy Section** — Education, healthcare, social welfare, and disaster relief
- **Quotes Section** — Six famous Ratan Tata quotes in glassmorphism cards
- **Legacy Section** — His impact on India, entrepreneurship, and ethical leadership
- **Gallery Section** — Six framed gallery panels with lightbox preview on click
- **Footer** — Respectful tribute message, credited to Mohsin Raza

---

## How to Run

1. Download or clone this folder
2. Open `index.html` in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
3. No server, no build step, no internet required after initial font load

The entire website is contained in a single `index.html` file.

---

## Technologies Used

| Layer | Details |
|---|---|
| Structure | HTML5 (semantic sections) |
| Styling | CSS3 — custom properties, glassmorphism, keyframe animations, clip-path, parallax |
| Interactivity | Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+) — IntersectionObserver, counter animations, parallax, lightbox |
| Typography | Playfair Display (headings) · Poppins (body) · Cormorant Garamond (quotes) via Google Fonts |
| Effects | Custom cursor, scroll progress bar, staggered reveals, 3D card hover, parallax scrolling |

No frameworks. No libraries. No build tools.

## Design Highlights

- **Luxury Corporate Legacy** aesthetic — inspired by Apple product pages and premium editorial magazines
- Gold (`#D4AF37`) accent palette on deep black (`#0A0A0A`) for a timeless, refined look
- Animated loading screen with RT monogram
- Custom gold cursor with smooth ring follower
- Scroll progress indicator at the top of the page
- Cinematic hero with parallax portrait area
- Timeline with alternating left-right card reveals
- Counter animations triggered on scroll into view
- Gallery with hover captions and full lightbox on click
- Fully responsive for mobile, tablet, and desktop
